About CogniScan

CogniScan is an innovative web-based application designed for early-stage cognitive screening. By combining storytelling with a series of interactive games, it provides a comprehensive and engaging way to assess various cognitive domains.

The Process

  1. User Information: The assessment begins by collecting basic demographic data like name, age, and education level, which are crucial for an accurate risk analysis.
  2. Storytelling Phase: The user is prompted to tell a story about a recent event. Our advanced AI transcribes the story and generates relevant follow-up questions.
  3. Cognitive Games: A series of four games evaluates different cognitive functions, such as memory, attention, and executive function.
  4. Story Recall Quiz: The user answers the AI-generated questions based on their initial story, testing their short-term memory and recall abilities.
  5. Cognitive Analysis: The application calculates a final score based on performance in all phases and provides a risk assessment based on established cognitive screening standards.

Why CogniScan?

Early detection of cognitive decline is crucial for timely intervention and management. CogniScan provides an accessible and user-friendly platform for individuals to monitor their cognitive health from the comfort of their own homes. It is a valuable tool for preliminary screening, encouraging users to seek professional medical advice if needed.
